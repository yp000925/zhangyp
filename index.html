About me

My research focuses on AI-powered computational imaging methods for inverse imaging problems, with interests spanning physics-informed machine intelligence, generative modelling, digital holography, and camera-based physiological sensing. I welcome academic collaborations on robust, interpretable, and deployable imaging systems for biomedical, environmental, and human-centred sensing applications.
